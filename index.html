Welcome to It's a Date

This site was created especially for those who are tired of taking their first dates to the same old pub, cinema, or nightclub. If you want to impress your date and win their affection, this site is here to help you find the perfect location within London. With multiple categories to explore, it should now be easy to create the most amazing date ever.

It's a Date was created by a group of girls from Code First: Girls, who want to make the world of dating fun again. This is an inspirational dating guide, so whether you're looking for a quiet, chilled environment or something more conventionally romantic, we have something for you.
